    About Me

    I am a Computer Science engineering student with a strong interest in machine learning, intelligent systems, and applied research. My work spans deep learning, computational imaging, NLP pipelines, and time series modelling. I enjoy solving technical problems, building practical systems, and exploring ideas that contribute to measurable progress in AI.

    I have contributed to several academic and innovation-driven projects, including developing object detection models for satellite imagery, creating financial forecasting pipelines. I have also been actively involved in entrepreneurship initiatives and was part of the winning team at the National Entrepreneurship Challenge at IIT Bombay.

    Alongside my technical work, I have a creative background shaped by two years of experience as a graphic designer. This blend of technical and creative skills helps me approach problems with clarity, structure, and a strong sense of design. I am committed to continuous learning and aim to build reliable and meaningful AI systems that can operate responsibly at scale.
