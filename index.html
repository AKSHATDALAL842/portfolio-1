    About Me

    Hey! I am a computer science student with a fervent enthusiast for machine learning. My fascination spans neural networks, deep learning, reinforcement learning, and the evolutionary reinforcement learning. Every new algorithm presents a challenge, and each breakthrough fuels my enthusiasm.

    One of my most rewarding experiences is reimplementing research papers. This hands-on approach not only deepens my understanding but also accelerates my learning journey. I believe that collaborating with a study partner makes the process even more effective and enjoyable.

    I am driven by a relentless curiosity and a desire to push the boundaries of machine learning. Experimenting with new ideas and solving complex problems keeps me engaged and motivated. But it’s not just about solo quests. I love teaming up with other researchers and fellow enthusiasts. There’s nothing better than brainstorming over a cup of coffee (or two) and cooking up the next big thing in AI together.
